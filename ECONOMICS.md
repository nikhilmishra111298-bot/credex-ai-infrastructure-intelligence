# Product Economics

## Overview

Credex AI Spend Audit is designed as a lead-generation and SaaS-style analytics platform focused on AI infrastructure optimization.

The business model evolves across multiple phases:
- free audit acquisition
- consulting/brokerage
- SaaS subscriptions
- infrastructure marketplace

---

# Cost Structure

## Infrastructure Costs

Current MVP costs are relatively low.

Main costs:
- Vercel hosting
- API usage
- analytics tooling
- email infrastructure

Estimated MVP hosting cost:
- minimal for early-stage usage

---

## AI Costs

AI usage is intentionally lightweight.

AI is used only for:
- summaries
- personalization

AI is NOT used for:
- pricing calculations
- heavy inference workloads

This keeps operational costs low.
