# AI Prompt Design

This document explains the prompts used for generating AI summaries in the Credex AI Spend Audit platform.

---

# Purpose

AI is used only for:
- narrative summaries
- contextual explanations
- personalization

AI is NOT used for:
- savings calculations
- financial decisions
- pricing math

---

# Summary Prompt Strategy

The application generates summaries using:
- team size
- use case
- savings opportunity
- optimization risk

The goal is to make the audit feel:
- personalized
- intelligent
- executive-friendly

---

# Current Prompt Inputs

Inputs:

- teamSize
- useCase
- totalMonthlySavings
- recommendations

---

# Personalization Dimensions

## Team Size

### Small Teams
Focus:
- avoiding enterprise overspend
- flexibility
- lean operations

### Mid-Sized Teams
Focus:
- duplicated subscriptions
- seat management
- collaboration costs

### Enterprise Teams
Focus:
- centralized procurement
- infrastructure governance
- large optimization opportunities

---

# Workflow Personalization

## Coding
Focus:
- developer tooling
- Copilot/Cursor optimization
- engineering workflows

## Writing
Focus:
- content workflows
- lightweight tooling
- avoiding engineering-focused plans

## Data
Focus:
- token/API usage
- scaling infrastructure costs

## Research
Focus:
- overlapping subscriptions
- experimentation spend

## Mixed
Focus:
- duplicated tooling
- operational inefficiency

---

# Example Summary

Example:

"Your growing engineering team may be overspending due to duplicated AI tooling and high spend-per-seat costs. Based on your coding-heavy workflow, optimizing developer-focused tooling and infrastructure purchasing could significantly reduce monthly spend."

---

# Why Use AI Summaries

Benefits:
- improves perceived intelligence
- creates executive-style reporting
- improves engagement
- improves SaaS product feel

---

# Risks Avoided

The system intentionally avoids:
- AI-generated pricing math
- hallucinated financial calculations
- unverifiable savings numbers

All financial outputs come from deterministic logic inside the audit engine.
