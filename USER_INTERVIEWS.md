# Credex — Interview Preparation Notes

# Project Introduction

## Short Introduction

Credex is an AI Infrastructure Intelligence Platform designed to help organizations analyze, optimize, and simulate their AI tooling ecosystem.

The platform predicts future AI infrastructure costs, identifies AI dependency risks, detects overlapping AI tools, and generates autonomous optimization recommendations.

It was built using:

* Next.js
* React
* TypeScript
* Tailwind CSS
* Supabase
* Recharts

---

# Interview Opening Answer

## Tell Me About Your Project

Credex is a futuristic AI Infrastructure Intelligence Platform that helps organizations understand and optimize their AI infrastructure.

The idea came from noticing that many companies use multiple AI tools like ChatGPT, Claude, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, and Perplexity without understanding future AI spending, infrastructure risks, or operational dependency.

I wanted to build something beyond a normal dashboard, so I created systems like:

* AI Spend Time Machine
* AI Tool DNA Mapping
* AI Risk Heatmap
* AI Infrastructure Collapse Simulation
* Autonomous AI CFO
* AI Personality Engine

The platform predicts future AI spend growth, identifies AI operational risks, and generates intelligent recommendations.

I built the frontend using Next.js, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS with a premium SaaS-style UI and Supabase backend integration.

# Major Features Explanation

# 1. AI Spend Audit Engine

Users can:

* add AI tools
* enter monthly spend
* define seat count
* choose plans
* define team size

The system calculates:

* total current spend
* monthly savings
* annual savings
* infrastructure health score

---

# 2. AI Spend Time Machine

This feature predicts:

* future monthly AI spend
* future infrastructure burn
* growth percentage
* scaling risk

It simulates:

> “What happens if the organization continues increasing AI usage?”

---

# 3. AI Tool DNA Mapping

This system classifies AI tools into categories:

* Core
* Overlap
* Overspend
* Efficient

The goal is to identify:

* duplicated AI tools
* inefficient subscriptions
* optimization opportunities

---

# 4. AI Risk Heatmap

The platform visually displays:

* Spend Growth Risk
* Tool Overlap Risk
* Seat Waste Risk

Each risk receives:

* numerical score
* Low / Medium / High classification
* visual progress bars

---

# 5. AI Infrastructure Collapse Simulation

This system predicts:

* productivity loss
* downtime cost
* vendor dependency exposure

The feature simulates operational AI failure scenarios.

---

# 6. Autonomous AI CFO

An AI-powered financial recommendation engine.

It generates:

* infrastructure optimization suggestions
* overspending reduction advice
* scaling efficiency recommendations

---

# 7. AI Personality Engine

This system profiles organizations based on AI infrastructure behavior.

Possible classifications:

* Lean AI Startup
* Balanced AI Team
* AI Hyper-Scaling Team
* AI Overspend Organization
