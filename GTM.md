# Go-To-Market Strategy

## Product Overview

Credex AI Spend Audit is a SaaS-style platform that helps startups and teams identify overspending across AI tools such as:
- ChatGPT
- Claude
- Cursor
- Copilot
- Gemini
- Windsurf

The platform estimates optimization opportunities and promotes discounted AI infrastructure purchasing through Credex.
